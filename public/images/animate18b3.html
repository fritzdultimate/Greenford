Transparency

The owners of sinopec global are convinced that the transparency and the access to information, play a vital role in building governments that are more open to the public scrutiny, able to promote a growing participation of the society in the design and evaluation of the public government policies, and, therefore, of a greater public accountability.

For the foregoing, the transparency policy of the Republic's Government has as purpose not only to contribute to the warranty of the right of access to the information of the Mexican people, but also to provide timely information that allows the improvement of decision making of the citizens and to improve the confidence and certainty about the government's action.

In this section you will find basic information on the policy of the Transparency of the Republic's Government, its objectives, scopes, the main results obtained from its implementation, as well as the challenges ahead.
